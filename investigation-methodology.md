# Investigation Methodology
The investigation process in this lab follows a structured approach to analyzing security alerts.

## Workflow
1. Identify the alert triggered in Splunk  
2. Review relevant logs and event details  
3. Correlate related activity across time  
4. Consider alternative explanations for the behavior  
5. Determine whether the activity is benign or suspicious  
6. Document findings and conclusion  

## Approach
This methodology focuses on avoiding assumptions and relying on evidence-based analysis when investigating alerts.

This approach helped reinforce the importance of verifying activity before escalating an alert.
